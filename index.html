Services

What we bring to the party

Themed Birthday Parties

We make your child’s favourite character or fantasy land come alive on their special day. Disney characters to football stadium, jungle safari to cars and bikes, our efforts are focused toward transforming the venue according to a wonderland.

Décor

Birthdays are special and no birthday party is complete without the birthday décor. We at Krimson Edge understand the value colourful balloons, streamers, ribbons and thermacols. Our range of decorations is high in quality and is as per your theme demands.

Games And Activities

At Krimson Edge we offer a wide range of activities to meet your child’s interests. Tattoo artist, magicians, bouncy, merry-go-round, photo corner, puppet show, pop corn maker, electric train, shooting games, nail art and a variety of games

Return Gifts

We offer planning and organizing of return gifts for different ages and in accordance with the theme of the party. We offer services in making of sweet bags and gift wrapping also.

About

We are organizers and planners of theme birthday parties for kids

  • Why Themed Birthday Parties For Kids?

    Young minds have fertile imaginations which is like a kaleidoscope, shifting focus as they move from year to year. Every girl or boy‘s fantasy is what we transform into reality, as the theme for that year’s most special day - their birthday. At Krimson Edge, we want to make this day the most memorable for our precious ones.

  • Hectic schedules, shortage of helping hands ,lack of extra time, make it tough to sit and plan this special birthday. We aim to ensure that your special child does not miss out on any aspect of his perfect fantasy world- WE CREATE YOU CELEBRATE!

  • Why Choose Us?

    We love birthdays and come up with innovative ideas to make your party fun. We understand your child’s fantasy world and love making it a reality.

  • Our clients have praised us for our efficiency and reasonable prices adjusting to your budget and party requirements.
